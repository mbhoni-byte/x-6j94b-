Meet Our Authors

The creative minds behind the haunting tales of dark romance that captivate your soul

M.B Makondo

Master of Romance Stories

M.B Makondo weaves intricate tales of forbidden love and eternal darkness. With a background in gothic literature, Makondo crafts haunting narratives that explore the deepest corners of the human heart. His stories blend poetic prose with chilling suspense, creating unforgettable journeys through the shadows of passion.

12 Published Stories

M.W Sithole

Poet of the Night

M.W Sithole brings a lyrical quality to dark romance, infusing each story with poetic beauty and emotional depth. Specializing in paranormal romance, Sithole creates complex characters who navigate the treacherous waters of immortal love. Her work is celebrated for its atmospheric settings and psychological complexity.
