Whispers in the Dark

How quickly do you respond to messages?

We typically respond to all messages within 24-48 hours. Your whispers in the dark are important to us, and we'll answer as soon as the moon rises again.

Do you accept story submissions?

Yes, we're always looking for stories to tell, even your story can be shared. Please send your submissions to our email, and our coven of storytellers will review them under the pale moonlight.

Can I request a specific type of story?

Absolutely. We thrive on the whispers of our readers. If there's a particular story you'd like to explore, share it with us, and we might just weave it into our next tale.
